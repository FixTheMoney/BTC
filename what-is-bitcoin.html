Desirable Properties of Money

  • Scarcity: Limited supply to maintain value
  • Durability: Ability to withstand repeated use
  • Portability: Easy to transport and transfer
  • Divisibility: Can be divided into smaller units
  • Fungibility: Each unit is interchangeable
  • Recognizability: Easy to identify and verify
  • Stability: Maintains value over time
